what is boots?

boots is a queer dance party hosted at assorted venues. it is hosted by chicago performers and artists abhijeet and kirk. i have been attending since 2022, and became a consistent attendee this year. my wife and i love this party and have had nothing but amazing times here!


why boots?

every time i have gone to boots, i have felt extremely loved, accepted, and seen. their harm reductive stance on drug use, their attentiveness to partygoers fun and safety, the incredible djs they feature... boots has quickly become my home and safe space to dance every month! i don't know where i'd be without this outlet to dress up and have fun.
