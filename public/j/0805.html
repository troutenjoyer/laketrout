what's floating, trout?

feeling very overwhelmed lately. i'm starting a partial hospitalization treatment in a week and i've been charging through website updates as a coping mechanism for how scared i am. it's funny, part of what i explained about going into treatment was how much i struggled with doing things i enjoy. and i still am. except coding and website design.
i'm really really scared of being too vulnerable online. i like behind the scenes coding because i dont have to talk to anybody or process my thoughts or feelings or the things in my head. this journal entry is very a la exposure therapy, reminding myself that a degree of vulnerability is important to allow people to be close to you. i want to have people close to me.

things i am going to work on in treatment, whether i like it or not

  • addressing my trauma and how it continues to impact my behavior
  • get on a med regimen that, while not entirely sedating, helps me function on a daily basis
  • advocating for myself inside and outside of treatment- what i need, what i want, and compromise that doesnt completely devalue myself.
  • understanding how to ask for reassurance from psychotic thoughts/beliefs/visions without fear of judgement, and identifying people i can safely do this with
  • restore bonds with current friends and work on feeling safe and secure in my relationships
  • find self soothing techniques and restore my relationship to previous self soothing techniques

creative pursuits i am going to return to
  • drag/performance, digital and irl
  • sewing
  • comics
  • collages
  • writing
  • music

accountability works! now i've bullied myself into putting my treatment goals into the silly little website i find myself stuck on constantly. please do not expect regular updates on treatment, i have no interest in doing that! i want to keep my privacy and safety with regards to this process, i just needed to get something in code because this is where i've been spending all my time as i prepare.
i am optimistic for how this process will improve my life. i can tell my wife if i feel unsafe in the program. we also have a really good doctors appointment lined up... im manifesting. im thinking. im listening and learning and processing. thank u for listening meow.
